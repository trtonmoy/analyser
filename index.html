About Us

0xAnalyser bot is a Telegram wallet tracker bot which provides traders with a comprehensive overview of their current and previous trades since starting the bot. The bot allows for users to view their current and previous transactions along with their profits and losses all in one place, allowing them to get a better understanding of how much they're actually making or losing in their portfolio. 0xanalyser bot seamlessly integrates data from both UniSwap and PancakeSwap so that users can track their history across multiple chains, further improving their trading proficiency.

Why 0xAnalyser bot?

To use 0xAnalyser, message the bot /wallet followed by your ERC-20/BEP-20 wallet address and the bot will automatically begin scanning your wallet for new transactions.
